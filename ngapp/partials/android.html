ZongList for Android lets you download your music to your device with one tap. Follow the instructions below to install the application on your device.

1. Download the application


Enter the link below on your Android device to download the application.

http://goo.gl/zfl28h

2. Update your device settings


In order to install the application you need to update your security settings to allow installation for untrusted sources

Go to Settings, tap Security and then check Unknown Sources

3. Install ZongList


Now you are ready to install ZongList. Go to your Downloads folder and tap on the zonglist.apk file in order to install it on your device

4. Login


Everything is ready now!

Just login with your account and download your music!
